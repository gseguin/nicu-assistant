## Function-Binding Pattern Established (codebase first)

This plan establishes the Svelte 5.9+ function-binding 2-arg form as a usable pattern in this codebase. Prior usage was zero (per RESEARCH §D-09 grep). The canonical shape locked in:

```svelte
<SelectPicker
  bind:value={
    () => pertState.current.medicationId ?? '',
    (v: string) => { pertState.current.medicationId = v === '' ? null : v; }
  }
  options={medicationOptions}
/>
```

The PARENS around the inner pair are required by the Svelte parser; the comma between getter and setter is the delimiter (see RESEARCH Pitfall 1). Future refactors of feeds/fortification/etc. SelectPicker bridges (deferred per D-07) can copy this form verbatim.
