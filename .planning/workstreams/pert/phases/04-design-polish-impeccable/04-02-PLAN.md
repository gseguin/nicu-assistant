## Auto-disposition rules (codifies CONTEXT D-03 -- Wave 2 plans MUST honor)

For each finding row in 04-FINDINGS.md:

| Severity | Default disposition | Override conditions |
|----------|---------------------|---------------------|
| P1       | **fix-now** (no exceptions) | None. Block phase closure until 0 P1 remain. |
| P2       | **fix-now** if <=5 LOC AND files affected subset of PERT-route allowlist; **defer** otherwise | Touches `src/lib/shared/components/**` -> forced **defer**. Touches `src/app.css` outside `.identity-pert` block -> forced **defer**. |
