# Phase 42.1 Research

**Researched:** 2026-04-24

## Implementation Approach Summary

Phase 42.1 is a **codification + enforcement sweep** of DESIGN.md across the 5 shipped calculators plus chrome. The 33 locked decisions split cleanly into a small number of **architectural seeds** (`<HeroResult>`, `<DisclaimerBanner>`, `formatMg`, scoped transitions, Identity-Inside chrome cleanup) followed by **per-calculator refactors** that consume those seeds. The dependency shape is a wave-into-fan: Wave 1 (chrome alignment) and Wave 4-bottom (decoration removal + utilities) can land first since they touch shared files; Wave 2 introduces the `<HeroResult>` shared component then **fans out** to every calculator (D-07, D-22, D-23, D-24, D-26 all consume it); Wave 3 (banner refactor) is independent of calculators and can run in parallel with Wave 2; Wave 5 is residue cleanup that runs anytime after the chrome and component foundations are in place.

The single most invasive decision is **D-08 (hero-fills-viewport + inputs-as-sticky-drawer)**. It is a structural redesign of every calculator route shell and changes the user's first-paint mental model. Recommend treating D-08 as its own plan, scheduled AFTER `<HeroResult>` ships and consumed across all calculators (otherwise we'd refactor every calculator twice — once into HeroResult, once into the new sticky-drawer shell). Also recommend gating D-08 behind a localStorage flag for our own QA before it goes universal — see Risks section.

Recommended wave structure: **6 plans across 5 waves**. Plan 1 (chrome + clearance) is independently shippable and unblocks user-visible value immediately. Plan 2 (`<HeroResult>` + 5-calculator refactor + decimal precision) is the core consolidation and ships the most lines of code. Plan 3 (disclaimer banner) is independently shippable. Plan 4 (decoration removal + scoped transitions + redirect) is mostly subtractive and low risk. Plan 5 (D-08 hero-fills-viewport + sticky drawer) is the structural redesign — depends on Plan 2. Plan 6 (residue + spec amendments + tests harness for HeroResult/formatMg/banner-migration) wraps verification and documentation. Plans 1, 3, 4 can run in parallel; Plans 2 → 5 → 6 are serial.
