## Outcomes

All 13 requirements shipped as defined. Two post-ship adjustments bundled:

1. **`showRangeError` prop on NumericInput** (commit `2c6d3e6`). Complement to `showRangeHint` from v1.6. User noted the "Outside expected range — verify" advisory felt noisy on Starting Volume (soft clinical range, not a hard validation). Added a second opt-out prop. Formula Starting Volume now passes both `showRangeHint={false}` and `showRangeError={false}`. The min/max are still enforced by the scroll-wheel handler.
2. **Removed two Fortification advisory a11y tests** (commit `2ab5ae7`). Fortification has only one NumericInput (Starting Volume), which now opts out of the advisory. The equivalent coverage lives in Morphine (weight/dose/percent all still render the advisory in both themes). Playwright a11y count: 12 → 10. Intentional, not a regression.
