# Plan 42.1-04: Decoration Removal + Scoped Transitions + Server Redirect

## Objective

Subtract decoration that undermines clinical confidence: delete the dock-style scroll magnification from BOTH morphine schedule (Phase 5 origin) AND GIR titration grid (Phase 33 clone — extended per RESEARCH finding 2 since DESIGN.md's "no scroll-driven animation" rule applies uniformly), narrow `app.css`'s global `html:not(.no-transition) *` to specific selectors (D-17 — reduces paint cost on slow devices), verify keep-list animations still gate on `prefers-reduced-motion` (D-18 — verification only), and replace JS `goto('/morphine-wean')` on `/` with a static `<meta http-equiv="refresh">` redirect that fires before JS hydration. Ships in Wave 4 — independent of HeroResult, parallel-safe with Plans 1 and 3.

**Critical RESEARCH finding 3 (D-19 redirect risk):** adapter-static `fallback: 'index.html'` means a deep link to `/uac-uvc` would also serve `index.html` (the prerendered `+page.svelte`). Without a pathname gate, the meta refresh would yank users from `/uac-uvc` to `/morphine-wean`. Plan 4 must add a `<script>` pathname guard.
