## Source-of-truth formula (locked)

From `morphine-wean-calculator.xlsx` Sheet1 B15..B24, for inputs weight=3.1, maxDose=0.04, decreasePct=0.1:

| Step | doseMg | doseMgKgDose | reductionMg |
|------|--------|--------------|-------------|
| 1 | 0.124 | 0.04 | 0 |
| 2 | 0.1116 | 0.036 | 0.0124 |
| 3 | 0.0992 | 0.032 | 0.0124 |
| 4 | 0.0868 | 0.028 | 0.0124 |
| 5 | 0.0744 | 0.024 | 0.0124 |
| 6 | 0.0620 | 0.020 | 0.0124 |
| 7 | 0.0496 | 0.016 | 0.0124 |
| 8 | 0.0372 | 0.012 | 0.0124 |
| 9 | 0.0248 | 0.008 | 0.0124 |
| 10 | 0.0124 | 0.004 | 0.0124 |

Reduction per step = `weight × maxDose × decreasePct` = `3.1 × 0.04 × 0.1` = `0.0124`. Linear. `calculateLinearSchedule()` already produces these values.
